 Get Started:-

 Entry:  	The starting point of this graph is known as an entry point. 
 Output: 	Where to bundle your application.
 Loaders:   Have all required files in your poject(webpack only understands JavaScript)
 			Identify files should be transformed by a certain loader and transform them
 			so that it can be added to your dependency graph.
 Plugin:    Plugins are mostly used performing actions and 			   	   
 			custom functionality on "compilations" or "chunks" of your bundled modules.
